# Sazio Support

Sazio is a food signal awareness and self-tracking app. It helps you notice patterns around food noise, cravings, snacking urges, automatic eating moments, and daily context.

## Data and Privacy

The current Sazio beta stores your logs locally on your device. It does not sync your logs to our servers.

If you delete the app, reset app data, clear device storage, or change devices, your local data may be permanently deleted unless you exported it first.

If future versions add accounts, syncing, analytics, paid features, or cloud storage, we will update our policy and terms before those features are released.

## Medical Disclaimer

Sazio is for personal awareness and self-tracking only. It is not medical advice, individualized nutrition advice, mental health care, eating-disorder care, diagnosis, or treatment.

For medical, nutrition, mental health, eating-disorder, or other health-related concerns, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

If you are in crisis or believe you may harm yourself, contact emergency services or a crisis support resource in your area.

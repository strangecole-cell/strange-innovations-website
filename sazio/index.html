SAZIO

Currently in beta

Track the signal, not the food.

Sazio is a food signal awareness app that helps you notice the patterns behind food noise, cravings, and automatic eating — without calorie counting or shame.

It is not a diet app. It is not a calorie counter. It is a self-tracking tool for awareness — designed to help you understand why eating feels easy some days and impossible on others.

# Sazio Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 23, 2026**

Strange Strategic Investments LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Sazio, a food signal awareness and self-tracking app.

Sazio helps users notice daily context and food-related signals such as food noise, cravings, snacking urges, automatic eating moments, sleep, stress, hydration, protein, fiber, and processed food exposure. Sazio is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, treatment app, diet program, calorie counter, or substitute for professional advice.

## Information Sazio Stores

In the current beta, Sazio stores information locally on your device using app storage. This may include:

- Daily Signal Check-In responses
- Food noise or signal logs
- Optional notes and tags
- Notification preferences
- Onboarding status

## Local-Only Beta

The current beta does not require an account and does not sync your logs to our servers. We do not receive your logs unless you choose to export or share them with us.

If future versions add accounts, syncing, analytics, paid features, or cloud storage, we will update this policy before those features are released.

## Analytics and Crash Reports

The current beta does not include third-party analytics or third-party crash reporting services. Apple may provide crash diagnostics to developers depending on your device and TestFlight/App Store settings.

## How Your Information Is Used

Sazio uses the data you enter to:

- Show your daily check-in information
- Save your signal logs
- Generate in-app patterns, summaries, and insights
- Let you view history and export your data
- Remember app preferences, such as notification settings

## Data Storage and Deletion

Your logs are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, reset app data, clear device storage, or change devices, your local data may be permanently deleted unless you exported it first.

You can reset local app data from within the app.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Because the current beta stores logs locally and does not use backend sync, we do not receive your logs unless you choose to export or share them.

## Health and Wellness Information

Sazio is for personal awareness and self-tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, individualized nutrition advice, mental health care, eating-disorder care, diagnosis, or treatment.

Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical, nutrition, mental health, eating-disorder, or other health-related concerns.

If you are in crisis or believe you may harm yourself, contact emergency services or a crisis support resource in your area.

## Children

Sazio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Strange Strategic Investments LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Strange Innovations apps, games, and digital products including but not limited to Food Noise, Cheat Code Mania, Race O Race, Element Quest, and Western Weston Education (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Services.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Services.

Food Noise — App-Specific Privacy

Information We Collect

The Food Noise app may collect the following information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including log entries, trigger records, and pattern data you voluntarily enter.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and improvement purposes.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to help us improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Food Noise app's core functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the app experience
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

Food Noise data is stored locally on your device by default. If cloud backup or sync features are introduced in future versions, you will be notified and given the choice to opt in.

Health & Wellness Data

Food Noise involves tracking personal behavior and eating patterns. This data is considered sensitive personal information. We do not sell, share, or monetize your health or wellness data with third parties. All behavior and tracking data entered into Food Noise remains on your device unless you explicitly share it.

Cheat Code Mania — App-Specific Privacy

Information We Collect

  • Game Progress: Scores, levels, and in-game achievements stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for compatibility purposes.
  • Analytics: Anonymized data about which game modes are played most frequently.

Children's Privacy

Cheat Code Mania is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us at hello@strangeinnovations.com with any concerns.

Race O Race — App-Specific Privacy

Race O Race collects only game progress and device compatibility information stored locally on your device. No personal information is required to play. Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated.

Element Quest — App-Specific Privacy

Element Quest is an educational game. Data collected includes game progress, quiz results (stored locally for progress tracking), and anonymized usage analytics. No personally identifiable information is required to use this app.

Western Weston Education — YouTube Channel Privacy

The Western Weston Education YouTube channel is operated through YouTube/Google. When you interact with the channel (subscribe, comment, like), you are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not independently collect data through the YouTube channel beyond standard YouTube Analytics, which provides aggregate, anonymized viewership data.

General Privacy Practices

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services including:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For app distribution. Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.
  • Analytics Providers: We may use privacy-first analytics tools that collect only anonymized, aggregated data.
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues. Crash reports do not contain personally identifiable information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Services or as required by law. App data stored locally on your device is controlled by you and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information. California residents may request disclosure of what personal information is collected, used, disclosed, or sold. To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@strangeinnovations.com.

International Users

Our Services are operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

App Store Compliance

Our apps comply with Apple App Store and Google Play Store privacy guidelines. Privacy nutrition labels and data safety forms are kept current in each app's store listing. We review and update these as our data practices evolve.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

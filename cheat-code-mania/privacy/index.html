Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Privacy Policy · Terms of Service · Support

Strange Strategic Investments LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Cheat Code Mania, a mobile game built by Weston Strange.

Cheat Code Mania is a cyberpunk platformer in which you play as a rogue hacker who uses legendary cheat codes as real superpowers across 50 handcrafted levels.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Cheat Code Mania does not require an account, login, or any personal details to play.

Game data stored locally on your device may include:

  • Game progress and level completion
  • High scores
  • Settings and preferences

This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Purchases

Cheat Code Mania is a one-time paid app. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy practices apply to any data collected during the purchase process.

Analytics and Crash Reports

Cheat Code Mania does not include third-party analytics or third-party crash reporting services. Apple may provide crash diagnostics to developers depending on your device and TestFlight or App Store settings.

Advertising

Cheat Code Mania contains no ads and does not use advertising identifiers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Because Cheat Code Mania stores game data locally and does not use backend accounts or sync, we do not receive your game data.

Data Storage and Deletion

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, uninstall and reinstall, or change devices without a backup, your local progress may be lost.

Children

Cheat Code Mania does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal information to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a new "Last updated" date. If future versions add accounts, cloud saves, analytics, or other features that affect data collection, we will update this policy before those features are released.

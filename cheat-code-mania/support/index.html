Purchase Issues

Cheat Code Mania is a one-time purchase through the Apple App Store. For purchase or billing issues, contact Apple Support directly — we do not process payments or issue refunds outside of Apple's systems.

Game Progress

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, change devices, or restore your device without a backup, your progress may not be recoverable. We recommend keeping device backups if you want to preserve your save data.

Reporting a Bug

If you encounter a crash or unexpected behavior, email us with:

  • A description of what happened
  • The level or section where it occurred
  • Your device model and iOS version

We appreciate every report — it helps Weston make the game better.
